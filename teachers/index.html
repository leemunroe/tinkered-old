What does TinkerEd do for educators?

  • TinkerEd empowers educators to collaborate with the creators of EdTech tools.
  • Educators gain free access to great EdTech tools.
  • Educators get paid for sharing their time and expertise.

“TinkerEd helps EdTech designers and engineers understand what teachers really want and what they will use. It helps them produce the best products to support teaching and learning. TinkerEd helps teachers by giving them a voice in product design and features. It makes our opinions matter! It is nice to receive a reward, but it is even better knowing you are making a difference for teachers and students!”

Heidi M, Teacher in Illinois
